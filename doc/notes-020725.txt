MCP3008 Connections:
VDD:  3.3V   (Pin 1 on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header)
VREF: 3.3V   (Pin 1 on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header)
AGND: GND    (Pin 6 on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header)
DGND: GND    (Pin 6 on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header)
CLK:  GPIO11 (Pin 23 on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header)
DOUT: GPIO9  (Pin 21 on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header)
DIN:  GPIO10 (Pin 19 on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header)
CS:   GPIO8  (Pin 24 on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header)

Raspberry Pi GPIO Header Pinout for SPI:
WHT 3.3V: Pin 1
GRY GND:  Pin 6
BRN MOSI: GPIO10 (Pin 19)
RED MISO: GPIO9 (Pin 21)
ORG SCLK: GPIO11 (Pin 23)
YEL CE0:  GPIO8 (Pin 24)
